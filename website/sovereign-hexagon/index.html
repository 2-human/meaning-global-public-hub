The framework

The Sovereign Hexagon

The foundational architecture of the New Earth Economy: six dimensions of a meaningful, sovereign human life. A brand can create value across each one. Most create value across a few, and leave the rest on the table.

Select a pillar to jump to it, or start with the audit that maps your brand across all six.

The six pillars

Each pillar is a place a brand can create human value.

Every pillar pairs a dimension of a sovereign life with the industries that serve it, and its own quality of light. The audit scores a brand against all six. Open any pillar to see the dimension in full.

01

Health

Grounded meaning · Life-force restoration

Where a brand restores the body’s baseline: health protocols, healing, somatic medicine, pharma, fitness and wellness. The value created is the capacity to function and feel well.

Open the pillar →
02

Nurture

Embodied meaning · Nourishment

Where a brand nourishes: high-quality food, functional nutrition and beverage, clean supplementation and the research behind them. The value created is daily well-being, felt in the body.

Open the pillar →
03

Wealth

Materialized meaning · Financial solvency

Where a brand builds material security: the new personal economy, wealth creation, finance, banking and investment. The value created is agency, the freedom that solvency buys.

Open the pillar →
04

Connection

Shared meaning · Self-sufficiency

Where a brand connects people to each other and to systems that serve them: value ecosystems, technology, AI, conscious media and clean energy. The value created is belonging.

Open the pillar →
05

Creation

Meaning creation · Self-realization

Where a brand enables self-realization: luxury, design, fashion, leisure and new materials. The value created is identity. This is the pillar most associated with our founding work on luxury.

Open the pillar →
06

Longevity

Future-proofed meaning · Full human potential

Where a brand extends and expands a life: longevity protocols and labs, biotech, human-tech and research. The value created is time, and the potential a longer, healthier life unlocks.
