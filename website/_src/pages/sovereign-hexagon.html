The six pillars

Each pillar is a place a brand can create human value.

Every pillar pairs a dimension of a sovereign life with the industries that serve it, and its own quality of light. The audit scores a brand against all six. Open any pillar to see the dimension in full.

Health

Grounded meaning · Life-force restoration
