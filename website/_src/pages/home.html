The shift

The economy is moving from extraction to meaning.

For a century, business treated people as consumers and value as something to extract. That model is running out of road. Trust is thinning, differentiation built on scarcity is weakening, and the brands that will compound value from here are the ones that create real human value, not just capture attention.

We call the replacement Humanistic Capitalism: an economy where a business grows by growing the people it serves. The mechanism is meaning. The map is the Sovereign Hexagon.
